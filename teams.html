Meet the Teams

Due to the large amount of teams that entered into the championship, I'll just be covering the top eight teams competing this year. These teams were selected due to their prominence in the R6 community and past performance. If the team has a website, I have provided the link to it in their bio.

TSM

TSM contains the players Achieved, Beaulo, Merc, Chala, and Geometrics. They are based out of Los Angeles, California and compete in several Esports including R6S, LoL, PUBG, Smash, and many more.

DarkZero Esports

DarkZero is a full-scale esports organization that combines state-of-the-art training and development. With a deep passion for gaming they strive to empower their players and to create innovative ways to watch and engage them and their fans. DZ contains the players Mint, Skys, Hyper, Eclipse, and NJR.

Spacestation Gaming

SSG contains the players Bosco, Rampy, Fultz, and ThinkingNade. They recently lost their fifth player and use a temporary replacement. Some of SSG previous achievements include 2nd place at OGA Pit Croatia Minor, United States Nationals 2019 Champions, Six Invitational 2020 World Champions, and Pro League Season 11 NA Champions.

Oxygen Esports

Oxygen Esports, the largest multi-title esports organization in New England, was born out of a strategic partnership between the founders of Helix Esports, Team Genji, and Team Reciprocity. Established in Boston in 2020, Oxygen Esports is breathing new life into the esports landscape by leading with a data-driven approach to talent acquisition and fanbase engagement that has a proven track record of success. O2 contains the players LaXinG, FoxA, VertcL, Yoggah, and Kyno.

Disrupt Gaming

Disrupt Gaming is a world class digital media and entertainment company. Disrupt contains the players Iconic, Shuttle, Retro, J90, and Shark.

Mirage

Mirage Esports is a professional Esports team dedicated to providing top notch content and competing with the best players around the world in tournaments. Mirage contains the players Cold, Arctic, Timian, Kenzoy, and Cookie with several standbys.

BeastCoast

Beastcoast's goal is to provide an environment that emphasizes quality content, entertainment and community engagement while keeping the focus on the players overall well-being and happiness. BC contains the players Phozzo, Ghxst, AnthonyMGS, Rooty, and Sippin.

Soniqs

The Susquehanna Esports team, or Soniqs for short, is a relatively new team formed recently that competes in multiple Esports including R6S, Rocket League, and PUBG. SQ contains the players Easilyy, Slebben, Gomfi, Iconic, and Supr.
